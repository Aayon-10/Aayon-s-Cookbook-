My Cookbook

Vegetarian Recipes -

Veggie Stir-fry

Ingredients: Carrots, Broccoli, Bell Peppers, Soy Sauce, Olive Oil

Instructions: Stir-fry the vegetables in olive oil, add soy sauce, and cook until tender.

Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Ingredients: Bread, Cheese, Butter

Instructions: Butter the bread, place cheese between slices, and grill until golden brown.

Pesto Pasta

Ingredients: Pasta, Pesto Sauce, Cheese,

Instructions: Boil the pasta, add the sauce in a pan, add pasta in the sauce and top it with cheese.

Baked Vegetables

Ingredients: Vegetables, Cheese, White Sauce

Instructions: Saute the vegetables in butter, add the sauted vegetables in a baking tray with white sauce, and top it with cheese and bake.

Non-Vegetarian Recipes -

Chicken Curry

Ingredients: Chicken, Onion, Tomato, Spices

Instructions: Cook onions, add chicken and spices, simmer with tomatoes until cooked through.

Chicken Tacos

Ingredients: Chicken , Taco Shells, Lettuce, Cheese, Salsa

Instructions: Cook the chicken, fill taco shells, and top with lettuce, cheese, and salsa.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients: Chicken Tikka, Makhani Gravy, Cream , Butter

Instructions: Make the makhani gravy, add chicken tikka to the gravy, and top with fresh cream and butter.

Fish Fry

Ingredients: Fish, Masalas, Lemon

Instructions: Clean the fish, marinate the fish with masalas and lemon, shallow fry in pan until golden brown.
